About Author
Sri Charan Lakkaraju

We search for role-models everywhere without realising there’s a major role-model right where we grow up, right with us from the very beginning. Our fathers.

My father’s role, his actions, his triumphs, his mistakes, his words, they all contributed to my growth as a person and as an entrepreneur. He has taught me a lot in life and he has done a lot of things that have taught me and shaped me into the person that I am today and yet I am nothing like him. It is also what he wanted of me.

overview

inside the book

When a child is born, a father is born too. As much as a child learns from a father, a father also learns from the child. This book covers what a person usually experiences with his/her father and the learnings a father and child give each other, in addition to the joys, tears, mistakes and triumphs…

Chapter 1

A father always teaches a child values and the importance of crucial aspects of life like truth and honesty.

Chapter 2

A father continuously challenges his child, making the child want to achieve more and more and strive for excellence.

Chapter 3

A father doesn’t want his child to fail but is always ready to support the child’s failure and hold the child’s hand out of failure.

Chapter 4

A father conditions a child towards what is right in an indirect manner, by creating an environment and enabling the child to learn.

Chapter 5

A father teaches the child the importance of having a sense of self-worth and self-esteem and makes the child realise them.

Chapter 6

A father always strives hard to give the child what he didn’t have himself, opportunities and more.
